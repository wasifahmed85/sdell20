When someone we love dies, we all need time to grieve. But death can come with a lot of hard work. We're here to help take some of the stress off your plate.

Do you need probate?

Probate is the legal process of dealing with someone's property, money and possessions after they die. Probate is only required in around 50% of cases.

Unsure if you need probate? Answer a few simple questions or call us now on 020 8068 9990 to speak to a probate specialist.
