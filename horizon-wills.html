Craft & technology

The Horizon method

We pair discreet human interviews with proprietary workflow tech. Every milestone is time-stamped, version-controlled, and shareable with the inner circle you nominate.

01. Discovery ateliers

Design-led interviews uncover values, guardianship wishes, and edge cases before drafting begins.

02. Scenario modelling

We stress-test multi-generational scenarios with our estate analytics engine and share a visual plan.

03. Concierge execution

Witnessing, notarisation, asset schedules, and secure uploads handled without you leaving home.

04. Ongoing stewardship

Annual life-audit, bereavement hotlines, and instant amendments via encrypted vault messaging.
